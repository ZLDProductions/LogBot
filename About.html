About LogBot

LogBot performs several tasks every time a message is sent or an event occurs. Because of this, I have been forced to split it up into at least 11 separately running programs to keep it fast. This is understandable, as the bot has over 100 commands.

The bot stores data for future review by the server admins. It was originally created to perform this task and this task only, but it has since then changed into something even better: a multipurpose bot that makes things generally easier to do.

There's a built-in bible (no copyright versions), a swearing filter, a dictionary, Wikipedia, Wolfram|Alpha, polling, a levels module, lots of security, message logging, and more.
