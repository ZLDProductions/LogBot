LogBot ToS

This bot follows Discord's Terms of Service.
Because of this, I am required to state exactly what information the bot stores.
The bot stores only information necessary to continue running the bot. It stores logs of every channel in a server, but they are not able to be reviewed by others, except for the admins of the server, and me.
I will not (and have not) peeked into any logs of a server I am not in. Logs include status changes, server changes, channel changes, message actions, and other publicly available data (data users can get from the Discord app), nothing else.
The bot also stores minor data about the frequency of activity (your rank/tier/etc.).
In addition, by using this bot, you agree that you will not abuse commands (spam, or give parameters to test just how far it can go) in any way, and that storing the information mentioned above is perfectly fine.
