LogBot ToS

This bot follows Discord's Terms of Service (ToS).
Because of this, I am required to state exactly what information the bot stores.
The bot stores only information necessary to continue operation. It stores logs of every channel in a server, which are accessible only by the admins of the server, through the `$fetch` command. Logs include status changes, server changes, channel changes, message actions, and other data available to those who use the Discord app, nothing more.
The bot also stores some data regarding frequency of activity (such as your rank and tier).
